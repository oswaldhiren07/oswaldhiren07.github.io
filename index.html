Background

"I build things — from hydroponic systems to data models — and figure out how to scale them."

Born and raised in Ludhiana, India, I completed my high school education at Sacred Heart Convent School before relocating to Penn State University in Pennsylvania, where I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computational Data Science in the College of Engineering.

My background spans sustainable agriculture, entrepreneurship, and engineering. I founded OD.Hydroponics at 16 — directing research, engineering nutrient delivery systems, and running community education programs. That early experience of building something from zero shaped how I approach every project: with ownership and precision.

I'm driven by the question of how emerging technology and data systems can solve real-world infrastructure problems, and I'm steadily building the technical and strategic foundation to lead at that intersection.
